We support two kinds of transactions. One is the basic transaction and another is transaction with smart contract. For the basic one, you need to provide payer's and receiver's address with your public + private key for a transaction. And if you want to make transaction with smart contract, we provide API for you to upload your contract and data. You can deploy it first and then apply it on the transaction.
